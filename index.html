The Physical Chemistry community at Purdue University prides itself on maintaining a culture of outstanding scientific achievement and close-knit collegiality. PChem research at Purdue spans orders of magnitude in both size (ultracold atoms to living cells) and time (femtosecond spectroscopy to cellular growth curves).

For more information about PChem research at Purdue check our research page. Check the education page for an overview of core PChem courses at Purdue. Or browse the posts below to learn more about upcoming events. And thanks for visiting!
