Workshop Program

Topics for this year's workshop include:

  • Quantum Chemistry and Electronic Structure (chair: Mike Reppert)
  • Quantum Computing and Quantum Networks (chair: Shengwang Du)
  • Quantum Measurements and Quantum Sensing (chair: Hadiseh Alaeian)
  • Quantum Optics (chair: Valentin Walther)
  • Quantum Materials (chair: Laimei Nie)

Each topic session will open with a pedagogical overview to the topic, followed by two shorter "research" talks illustrating the application of those concepts in current research. During the two Core Skills sessions on Saturday afternoon, attendees can choose to attend any two of three breakout session options.
