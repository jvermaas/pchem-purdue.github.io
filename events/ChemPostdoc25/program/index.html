Program

The symposium will feature research talks from the Purdue Chemistry postdoctoral community, along with two invited seminars:

  • Dr. Christopher Welch (Indiana Consortium for Analytical Sciences and Engineering): "Launching an innovation-focused career in industry"
  • Dr. Garth Simpson (Purdue University): "The post-doc and Beyond: An Unofficial Guide to Tenure"

The event schedule is as follows:

  • 10:30AM-11:00AM.   Sign in
  • 11:00AM-11:30AM.   Welcome talk and committee member introductions
  • 11:30AM-12:30PM.   Invited speaker talks
  • 12:30PM-13:30PM.   Lunch time
  • 13:30PM-15:00PM.   Postdoc talks
  • 15:00PM-16:00PM.   Poster section
  • 16:00PM-16:20PM.   Panel talk
  • 16:20PM-16:30PM.   Awards Session!
