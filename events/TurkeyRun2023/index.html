Welcome to Turkey Run!

We are excited to announce the 49th Midwest Southeast Turkey Run conference to be held at the Turkey Run Inn at Turkey Run Start Park in Marshall Indiana. For nearly 50 years, the Midwest Southeast conference has served as an important venue to discuss new developments in photosynthesis research, from ultrafast spectroscopy to plant biology.

An important goal of the MWSE PC is to provide a venue for undergraduate, graduate, and postdoctoral researchers to present and showcase their work and to meet and discuss research with leaders in their field. The beautiful scenery and hiking trails at Turkey Run State Park help to create a welcoming and inclusive atmosphere for conversation and collaboration.

This year we are excited to welcome Jennifer Bridwell-Rabb and Himadri Pakrasi as keynote speakers.
