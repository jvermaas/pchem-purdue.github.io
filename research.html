Purdue Physical Chemistry prides itself on its culture of outstanding scientific research and close-knit community.

Quantum Information Science

Purdue PChem has both a long history and a growing community of quantum information research, from developing new quantum-computing algorithms to exploring new quantum materials and building new experimental platforms for quantum state manipulation.


Quantum Chemistry

Electronic structure and quantum chemistry efforts at Purdue span the full range fromfundamental research on DFT functionals to full-protein quantum mechanics/molecular mechanics (QM/MM) simulations and stochastic methods for scalable DFT.


Ultrafast Science

Ultrafast microscopy and ultrafast XUV/X-ray spectroscopy research at Purdue explores the fundamental properties of excitonic materials and the light-matter strong coupling regime.


Photosynthesis Research

Photosynthesis research at Purdue is driven by a potent combination of experimental mutagenesis work, ultrafast spectroscopy, and first-principles quantum and molecular dynamics.


Data Science

Data science research in Purude PChem range from immunology and drug discovery to enhanced image sampling for ultrafast microscopy and data-driven molecular dynamics simulations.
